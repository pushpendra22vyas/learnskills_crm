SPORTS

Physical recreation through games and sports has been an important part of the Institute since its inception. Games like cricket, football, volleyball and basketball are the part of college activities since its establishment years. Today, the Institute has the best facilities of basket ball, tennis, table tennis, cricket and football. The tradition continues, as students of the institute keep making use of the sports facilities to excel in sports and games. In order to motivate the students and to get them interested in sports, an annual staff-student cricket match is the integral part since its inception.
