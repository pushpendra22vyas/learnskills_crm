College Library

The esteemed institute has a well furnished library with more than 18000 books covering more than 6400 titles including text books, excellent reference books by renowned and established authors, all classified and easily accessible. Library is equipped with RFID (Radio Frequency Identification) facility and Computer Section with accessibility of e-contents established with the help of the financial grant received under Plan (Chief Minister Budget Announcement) sanctioned by Government of Rajasthan).

There is a reading room provided with a number of national and international magazines, journals and newspaper. These facilities are ever expanding and there is a regular flow of books and journals for the purpose of reading and learning. Library has been properly classified with books and racks. The students can also have access to computer network in the library area.
